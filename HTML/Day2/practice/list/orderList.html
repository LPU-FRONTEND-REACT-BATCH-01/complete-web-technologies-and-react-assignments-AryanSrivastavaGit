clear, step-by-step method to prepare a classic chicken biryani at home 🍗🍚

🧂 Ingredients

  1. 500g chicken (cut pieces)
  2. 2 cups basmati rice
  3. 2 onions (thinly sliced)
  4. 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  5. 1 cup curd (yogurt)
  6. 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  7. Whole spices: bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom
  8. Powder spices: turmeric, red chili powder, biryani masala
  9. Fresh coriander & mint leaves
  10. 2–3 green chilies
  11. Salt (to taste)
  12. Oil + ghee
  13. Saffron (optional) soaked in warm milk
